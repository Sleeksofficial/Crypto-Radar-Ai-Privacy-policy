Privacy Policy for CryptoRadar AI Bot

Last updated: August 31, 2025

1. Information Collected

The Bot receives the text commands you send (e.g. /topcoins, /airdrops, /aipredict, /memecoins).

The Bot sees your Telegram user ID and chat ID to deliver responses.

The Bot does not request or collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.

2. How We Use Data

We use this information to process your commands and return crypto information, predictions, and alerts.

Data may also be used to maintain service quality and troubleshoot errors.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your Telegram data with third parties.

External APIs (e.g. CoinGecko, CoinPaprika, OpenAI) are used to fetch information. Only your command text (not your Telegram ID) is sent to those services.

4. Data Retention

The Bot does not permanently store your messages or chat IDs.

Logs may be kept temporarily for debugging and are periodically deleted.

5. Your Rights

You may stop using the Bot at any time by removing it from your chats.

To request deletion of specific logs, contact the developer.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: Oldest2905 (bot publisher).
